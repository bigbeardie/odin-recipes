Warm chicken teriyaki salad

Description

Warm tender chicken coated in sweet teriyaki goodness, smooth and silky rice vermicelli, crisp shredded gem lettuce,

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons of sake
  • 2 Tablespoons of mirin
  • 2 Tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 Teaspoon of light brown sugar
  • 3cm Piece of ginger root, peeled and grated
  • 2 Galic cloves, peeled and finely chopped
  • 200g Dark chicken meat (leg or thigh), roughly chopped
  • 100g Rice vermicelli
  • 2 Teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1 Tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 2 Handfuls of beansprouts
  • 1 Head little gem lettuce, leaves separated and shredded
  • 1 Red chill, deseeded and finely chopped
  • 4 Tablespoons of frozen peas, defrosted
  • Bunch of coriander, leaves picked
  • Salt and white pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon of coarsely chopped salted peanuts

Steps

  1. Combine the marinade ingredients and gently heat to dissolve the sugar
  2. Allow to cool completely then combine with the chicken
  3. Set aside for 1 hour; over night in the fridge is even better
  4. Cook the vermicelli according to the instructions on the packet, drain and refresh under cold water
  5. Toss with the sesame oil
  6. Heat the oil in a hot wok over a medium heat
  7. Add the chicken and its marinade
  8. Cook for about four minutes until the meat is done and the liquid is reduced and thickened
  9. Remove from the heat
  10. Combine the noodles with the beansprouts, lettuce, cilli, peas and coriander leaves.
  11. Season with salt and pepper and toss to ensure that everything is well mixed
  12. Pile on to 2 plates, spoon over the chicken and it's juices and serve topped with the peanuts
